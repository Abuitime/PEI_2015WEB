About Us

The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE), the Society of Women Engineers (SWE) and the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) are pleased to invite you to join our Seventh Annual Professional Evening with Industry (PEI). Our three organizations have come together to create this exceptional annual event to build a bridge between all three sectors: the student, the professional, and the community.

Companies

Professionals will have the unique opportunity to meet talented Jacobs School of Engineering students including student leaders from SHPE, NSBE, and SWE. Industry representatives will also have the opportunity to host a booth to showcase their company and interact with students informally. Participating in this event will give industry professionals access to our next generation of talented engineers. Last year's PEI was attended by 19 sponsoring companies with close to 500 registered students. Deadline to register is September 26th or when we reach full capacity.
